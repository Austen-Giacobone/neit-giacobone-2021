Review

Austen Giacobone
Date:1/28/2021 Days of Thunder was a staple movie of my childhood.
My family being huge NASCAR fans, this movie played in our house on repeat all day.
Cole Trickle is a new young driver trying to make his name on the NASCAR circuit.
Team owner Tim Deland wants Cole to drive for him, but Cole needs a crew chief.
Harry Hodge retired the year before due to his last driver dying in a wreck at Daytona the previous year.
Tim convinces Harry to crew for Cole and race against his big rival, Rowdy Burns.
Rowdy and Cole get into a devastating wreck and are both sent to the hospital where Cole meets DR. Claire.
Since Rowdy is no longer able to race due to a brain injury, he supports Cole by giving him his car for the Daytona 500.
Cole goes on to win the Daytona 500.

Trivia

  • Production began without a finished script. Scenes were often written the day of filming.
    During one driving sequence, Tom Cruise actually had to read his lines off cue cards attached to his windshield, which resulted in a minor car accident.
    For subsequent driving sequences, Cruise was fitted with a special earpiece to have lines fed to him.
  • The movie was conceived by Tom Cruise when he and Paul Newman were allowed to test one of Rick Hendrick's race cars.
    Tom's first lap was in excess of one hundred eighty miles per hour.
  • According to Dale Earnhardt, Jr., Tom Cruise had a meeting with Dale Earnhardt, Sr. prior to the production of the film.
    It was rumored that Earnhardt was offered the role of Rowdy Burns, but he turned it down because he did not want to play the bad guy.
